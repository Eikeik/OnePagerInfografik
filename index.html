Climate Politics

Climate risk:
Insuring against the inevitable

Visual Journalism Team at DW

published 27.04.2019

This is the story of how, since the millennium, Europe has become an increasingly hostile environment for those of its residents who happen to have been born elsewhere.

We will also draw on the work of experts – many of whom are migrants themselves – to show how today’s xenophobia has rhetorical roots that can be traced back decades. How did marginal racist parties end up dictating the agenda for the European political mainstream – and increasingly threatening to take power?

Finally, we’ll meet the new Europeans fighting to defend the values of tolerance and freedom that the continent claims to hold dear.
